Features

User Personalized Attributes

Personalized Login & Tracking: Track your impact over time. Visualize how small, consistent actions lead to significant changes in your carbon footprint. Inspire action by linking personal efforts to broader environmental goals. Interactive Personalized Avatar: Create and customize an avatar that evolves with your progress. Gain accessories and upgrades by completing tasks and reaching sustainability milestones. Adds a gaming-touch to make it fun and engaging.

User Engagement: Weekly Challenges

Complete eco-friendly tasks like reducing energy usage or planting seeds for rewards.

Progress Tracking

Visualize your impact with engaging progress charts and milestones.

Your actions towards sustainability directly impact the earth. So.. your progress with tasks is directly reflected through the appearance of your Enviro-Friend!

Community Involvement

Educate yourself, Spread the word: Some tactics to a greener lifestyle are less obvious. Learn how big of an impact small daily actions have on our planet

Tasks revolving reducing carbon footprint: Be challenged to new, fun, innovative, easy ways to do your part in taking care of the environment

Compete with your friends: View the leaderboard to track other users task progress

Examples of Weekly Tasks

  • > Carpool, bike or walk to your next location!
  • > Use a re-usable shopping bag
  • > Cold wash and hang dry your laundry
  • > Conserve energy by keeping the lights off
  • > Use a re-usable water bottle
